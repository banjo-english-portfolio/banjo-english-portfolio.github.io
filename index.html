Prologue #4 - Loves, Likes, Dislikes #13 - All-Purpose-Excuse All-Purpose-Excuse Script

I’m Banjo; 17 years old and marginally incompetent. Throughout my life I have been driven by many little obsessions.

Beginning at age four, I was entranced by spider-man.

Everywhere I went I would climb on walls, and would ask for pictures of my spidey poses.

Until the age of seven I would assure everyone I really was him.

Shortly after realizing this was a fallacious theory, the age of dinosaurs began. Each project in school I warped to pertain to dinosaurs. Written assignments, my concours d’art oratoires french speaking presentations, even my maths tests were made to be, for three years, all about dinosaurs.

Recently though, the dinosaur phase elapsed, establishing a gap to employ the new, current obsession of software engineering.

Unlike the dinosaur fixation, this one is much more easily intertwined with classwork. It is for this fact, that I present to you, with slight sheepishness, my digital English 12 creative writing portfolio. This selection of artistic idiocy is a portrayal of myself, Banjo. First, a list of loves, likes, and dislikes, to get to know me, followed by something I should have written years ago, the ultimate all-purpose excuse.
